## The Challenge

Meridian Goods sells premium home goods direct to consumer. Three years in, the team had product-market fit, a passionate customer base, and a storefront built on a Shopify theme that had been forked, patched, and forked again until nobody on the team would commit to the codebase. Conversion rate sat at 1.4 percent. Mobile was worse. Organic traffic was flat. Repeat purchase rate was strong but unmeasured.

The new VP of Brand & Digital arrived with a clear mandate from the CEO: do not rebuild the brand from scratch, but make every customer surface look like the same company shipped it. And do it without breaking Q4, the quarter that pays for the whole year.

## Our Approach

We split the work into three tracks running in parallel: a storefront rebuild, a growth foundation, and a loyalty program. We embedded with the Meridian team three days a week and set one success metric per track. Conversion rate for the storefront. Non-brand organic sessions for growth. Repeat-customer revenue share for loyalty.

We rebuilt the storefront on Shopify Hydrogen with a typed component library mapped from a refreshed Figma system. The brand identity was tightened, not replaced — a refined wordmark, a tighter type scale, a two-color palette, and a photography direction that ruled out every stock photo the legacy site had been quietly relying on.

The growth track started with an honest SEO teardown. The legacy site had 1,400 indexable URLs, 600 of them duplicate or thin. We collapsed the catalog into a clean information architecture, shipped a programmatic collection layer, and rewrote the 80 pages that drove 90 percent of organic revenue.

The loyalty program was the riskiest bet. We designed it around a single insight from customer interviews: Meridian's most loyal customers wanted early access, not discounts. We shipped a tiered membership with early product drops, member-only colorways, and a referral mechanic that gave both sides a real benefit.

## What We Shipped

- A Shopify Hydrogen storefront with a typed component library, edge-rendered PDPs, and a 1.6s p75 LCP on mobile.
- A refreshed brand system: wordmark, type scale, photography direction, and a banned-words list every writer signs off on.
- A programmatic SEO layer covering 312 long-tail collection pages and a rewritten top-80 page set.
- A loyalty program with tiered membership, early access drops, and a referral flow integrated into the checkout.
- A lifecycle email overhaul with eight new flows, including a post-purchase sequence that pushed repeat purchase rate up 19 percent.
- A weekly performance and revenue dashboard wired to Shopify, GA4, and Klaviyo, reviewed live every Monday.

## The Outcome

Sitewide conversion rate moved from 1.4 percent to 3.6 percent within 90 days of relaunch and held there. Revenue per session climbed 157 percent. Non-brand organic traffic was up 184 percent six months in, driven by the new collection layer and the rewritten top pages. The loyalty program drove 31 percent of Q4 revenue in its first quarter live, with a 42 percent member referral rate.

Meridian crossed $2.4 million in annual recurring revenue six months after relaunch. The engagement extended twice — first to ship the wholesale portal, then to support the launch of a sister brand on the same platform we built.
